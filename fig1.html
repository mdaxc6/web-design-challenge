Max Temperature


The temperature becomes significantly warmer the closer a city is located to the equator. One interesting thing we observed was that the southern hemisphere seemed to be significantly warmer this time of year. This could be partily be due to the increased number of data points for the northern hemisphere. There are few cities in the dataset below -60 degrees lattitude, but there are numerous cities above 60 degrees lattitude. If you ignore points below -60, and above +60, the curve is much more consistant.
