City Latitude vs Humidity


The above plot is showing the relationship between latitude of a city and its humidity. The average humidity value seems to be a lot more sporadic. However, there does seem to be a higher concentration of cities with 100% humidity near the equator and northern hemispheres, but not as much in the southern hemisphere. This could be attributed to the higher number of measurements in the northern hemisphere when compared to the southern.
